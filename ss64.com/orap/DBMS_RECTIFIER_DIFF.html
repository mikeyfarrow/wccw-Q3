PL/SQL Packages DBMS_RECTIFIER_DIFF

Detect and resolve data inconsistencies between two replicated sites.

Subprocedures:

DIFFERENCES  Determine the differences between two tables. 

RECTIFY      Resolve the differences between two tables.  

For full documentation of the packaged procedures above see the Oracle Manual:
"Oracle9i Supplied PL/SQL Packages and Types Reference"

or the book Oracle Built in Packages by Steven Feuerstein et al
