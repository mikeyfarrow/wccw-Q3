RMAN completedTimeSpec

Subclause to specify a date range.

Syntax:

   COMPLETED AFTER [=] 'date_string'

   COMPLETED BETWEEN 'date_string' AND 'date_string'

   COMPLETED BEFORE [=] 'date_string'
