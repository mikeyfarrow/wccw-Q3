GPA Calculator

A 4 point grading scale is very precise, and makes it easy to find the average grade for all your courses, called a Grade Point Average. On this scale, a 4 is equivalent to percentage grade of 90-100%, 3 is equivalent to 80-89%, 2 is equivalent to 70-79%, and 1 is equivalent to 0-69% (not passing).

Enter each of your courses and the grade (between 1 and 4) that you recieved for it, then click "Calculate" to see your overall GPA.
